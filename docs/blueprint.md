# **App Name**: JEZZA COOKS

## Core Features:

- Service Route Cards: Display three service route cards (Consulting, Catering, Websites) on the homepage, each linking to its respective service page.
- Pricing Cards: Present pricing information in a card format, allowing for different tiers per service with included features.
- Contact Form Submission: Capture lead information, store it in Firestore, and trigger email notifications upon form submission.
- FAQ Accordion: Implement an FAQ accordion for common questions on the FAQ page, ensuring easy access to information.
- WhatsApp CTA: Add a floating WhatsApp button (mobile) for direct communication; it should use a placeholder ENV variable for the phone number.
- Insights Blog Article: Create and display a blog article focused on restaurant consulting, providing valuable insights and expert advice.
- SEO and Performance Optimization: Ensure SEO readiness, fast image loading, responsive design, and achieve a Lighthouse score of 90+ for optimal performance.

- Accent color 2: Paprika (#E26D2D) for highlighting important details.
- Accent color 3: Mint (#7BD7C0) used sparingly for fresh highlights.
